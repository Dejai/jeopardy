RULES

  1. Everyone gets a chance to answer every question!
         For each question, every team gets a chance to answer.
         Including the "Daily Double" questions

  2. During the regular rounds, you do NOT lose points for getting it wrong!
         Feel free to guess away without any penalty.
         You can only lose points during the FINAL JEOPARDY round.

  3. You've got 15 seconds!
         That's how long you get to deliberate for each question.
         Time starts after the question is read

  4. For FINAL JEOPARDY, you can wager as much as the
    HIGHEST OVERALL SCORE!
         So, you could come from behind & beat the team in the lead 🙂
         Or lose it all and end up with negative points. 🙃
